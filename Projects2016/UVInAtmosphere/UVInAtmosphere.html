This applet will walk you through the steps by which UV light is absorbed by the atmosphere. Once you can no longer click "Next" in the bottom right corner of the screen, move the photometer (The photometer is black and white, and it is located on the upper left corner of the atmosphere image.) down until you reach the next layer.

Don't forget to click on the various "show" buttons, as these will reveal additional key information and diagrams!

Applet Name: UV in the Atmosphere

Version/Date: 1.0.0, August 2015

Author(s): Marc-Olivier Lajeunesse, Tyler deBoon, Dr. Brian Martin, Dr. Peter Mahaffy
